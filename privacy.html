Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

ScaleLab AI ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website elevateonlinesuccess.com. This page explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Join our waitlist (email address)
  • Book a workshop (name, email, tool preferences, skill level, goals)
  • Take our quiz (responses are not stored)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Send you workshop invitations and updates via email
  • Personalize your workshop experience based on your skill level and tool preferences
  • Respond to your inquiries and requests
  • Improve our workshops and website

3. Email Communications

We use MailerLite to manage our email list. By submitting your email, you consent to receive workshop-related emails from us. Every email includes an unsubscribe link, and you can opt out at any time. We will never sell or share your email address with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Storage

Your information is stored securely through:

  • MailerLite (email marketing platform) for email addresses and names
  • Google Sheets (via Google Apps Script) for workshop booking details

Both services use industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

5. Analytics

We use Plausible Analytics, a privacy-friendly analytics tool that does not use cookies and does not collect personal data. It is fully compliant with GDPR, CCPA, and PECR.

6. Cookies

Our website does not use cookies for tracking. Plausible Analytics is cookie-free. Third-party services (MailerLite) may set their own cookies when you interact with embedded forms.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Unsubscribe from our emails at any time
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@lynnheidi.com.

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites (YouTube, Facebook, Instagram). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your personal data, contact us at:

support@lynnheidi.com
